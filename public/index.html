About

     Real-life decisions are rarely a simple calculation; they are often clouded by uncertainty, shifting environments, and human emotion. While traditional models assume we are perfectly rational, the reality is far more complex. Our interactive platform bridges this gap, offering a sandbox for human decision-making. Inspired by John Nash’s Game Theory and powered by AI reinforcement learning, the system allows you to test diverse strategies—from the purely rational to the emotionally driven—over time. Built with NodeJS and Python, and utilizing Google AI Studio, we transform abstract choices into visual data insights. Whether you are a student exploring career paths, a teacher fostering critical thinking, or a parent guiding a child, our platform helps you visualize the long-term consequences of every move.


Master the art of the long game and turn uncertainty into a strategic advantage.
